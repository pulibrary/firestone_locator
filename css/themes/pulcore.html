Q: Where is my barcode?

A: The barcode can be found on the back of your Princeton ID. The barcode is a 15 digit number, beginning with 22101.

Q: How do I select a pickup location?

A: Each item which may be requested has an option in the right column for selecting a pickup location. If you have selected an item, you simply need to select a pickup location. Note, some items have restricted pickup locations, the pickup option will reflect this.
